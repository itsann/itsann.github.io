What it does

The numbers you're holding in your head, held for you

A stash that stays honest

Log what you pump, where it goes, what baby eats. Fridge, freezer, deep freeze, daycare — reconciled, with expirations flagged before they bite.

Bring-to-destination math

NICU, daycare, grandma's — Milk Run knows what's already there and tells you exactly how much to bring to stay stocked through the week.

Trends that calm, not alarm

Supply moves in weeks, not days. Weekly trends, per-side patterns, and days-of-cover — framed to inform you, never to scare you.

Share the feeds

Invite your partner or a caregiver. Every bottle they give shows up on your phone, every pump you log shows up on theirs. The night shift finally has a handover document.
