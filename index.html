Pumping is hard.
Tracking it shouldn't be.

Milk Run keeps your pumping, nursing, and milk stash in one calm place — and does the math you're doing at 3am: how much to bring, how long you're covered, which side is next.

Coming soon — get launch email

Built for how feeding actually goes

  • Exclusively pumping — session goals, daily totals, and a bring-to-destination calculator for NICU, daycare, or grandma's that's right on the first day.
  • Breast and bottle — stash runway, a cover check for the day, and your at-breast balance over time.
  • Mostly nursing — when baby last fed and which side is next, remembered for you.
  • Twins — per-baby logs, next-side memory for each.

Your rhythm changes as your baby grows — switch modes any time and the whole dashboard reshapes itself.

Share the feeds

Invite your partner or a caregiver. Every bottle they give from the stash shows up on your phone; every pump you log shows up on theirs. The night shift finally has a handover document.

No guilt, by design

No streaks. No red numbers. A missed session never resets anything or scolds you. Built by a parent, for parents doing an unreasonably hard thing.

Private by default

Your data lives on your device — an account is only needed for sync and sharing. No ads, no trackers, no data selling, ever. Export everything to CSV whenever you like.
