Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

Milk Run tracks something personal — how you feed your baby. This policy is written to be read, not skimmed past. The short version: your feeding data lives on your device, we never sell data or show ads, and an account is optional.

What Milk Run stores, and where

On your device (always). Everything you log — pumping sessions, nursing sessions, feeds, your milk stash, notes, and any baby details you enter (a name and birthdate, if you choose to add them) — is stored locally on your phone. The app works fully without an account.

On our servers (only if you create an account). If you sign in to enable sync or family sharing, the same data you logged is copied to our database (hosted by Supabase) along with your email address, so your log can follow you to a new phone and appear on your partner's. If you never sign in, none of your feeding data leaves your device.

Anonymous usage analytics. To understand whether the app is working for new parents, we record a small set of anonymous events (for example: onboarding was started, which onboarding answers were chosen, the upgrade screen was shown). These events carry a random device identifier — never your email, name, account id, or anything you logged about your baby. They cannot be connected to your account.

Family sharing

Sharing is opt-in and invitation-based. If you invite someone, they can see — and, as a caregiver, add to — the feeding log you share. You can revoke access at any time in Settings, and invited members can leave at any time. Nobody can see your data without an invite you created.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to check your subscription status; they receive purchase receipts from Apple but none of your feeding data. We never see your payment details.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, ever, and no ad trackers.
  • No selling or renting data to anyone.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs — the anonymous events above go only to our own database.
  • No reading your health records — Milk Run only knows what you type into it.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app deletes everything stored on your device. If you created an account, you can delete the account and every synced record from Settings, or email itsannlin@gmail.com and we will delete it within 30 days.

Your rights (GDPR & similar laws)

If you're in the EU/EEA, UK, or a jurisdiction with similar privacy laws: we process account data because you asked us to provide sync (performance of a contract), and anonymous usage events on the basis of legitimate interest — they contain no personal data. You have the right to access, correct, export, and erase your data: export lives in the app (Settings → Export), erasure lives in the app (Settings → Delete account) or by email, and access/correction requests can go to itsannlin@gmail.com — we respond within 30 days. Synced data is hosted by Supabase; you can also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children

Milk Run is for parents and caregivers — adults. It stores information about your baby only because you choose to enter it, and that information is yours to delete at any time.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes materially, we'll note it in the app before the change takes effect. Questions: itsannlin@gmail.com.
