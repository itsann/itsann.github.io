Milk Run is not medical advice

Milk Run is a tracking and planning tool. Its numbers — intake estimates, storage guidance, "days of cover," suggested targets — are general calculations based on what you enter, not clinical guidance for your baby. Milk storage durations in the app reflect common public guidelines and can be wrong for your situation (prematurity, illness, fortified milk, and more all change the rules). Always follow the instructions of your pediatrician, lactation consultant, or NICU care team over anything this app shows. If your baby seems unwell, contact a medical professional — not an app.
